Hi, I'm

Gianna-Carina Grün

As a data-journalist, I turn datasets into stories that matter. I'm mainly working for DW Data, the data-driven journalism team that I founded and run since 2017. Beyond that, I also work as a data journalism trainer for both experienced journalists as well as journalist trainees. In my spare time, I'm pursuing a PhD investigating how well elaborate chart types are understood – and how we might improve that as practitioners.
